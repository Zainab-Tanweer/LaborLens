How Classification Works

The AI model automatically classifies companies based on 30 features including:

  • Production Data: Monthly production, item prices, release cycles
  • Environmental Impact: Carbon emissions, water usage, waste generation
  • Labor Conditions: Worker wages, working hours
  • Business Metrics: Return rates, customer spending, shopping frequency
  • Social Sentiment: Social media mentions, public sentiment analysis
  • Compliance Scores: Sustainability, transparency, compliance, ethical ratings

The model provides a confidence score indicating how certain it is about the classification.
